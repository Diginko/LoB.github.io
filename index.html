How does Solo boost differ from Duo boost?

Solo boost is a service where a booster logs into your gaming account and plays the game on your behalf to increase your account's ranking. On the other hand, Duo boost is a service where you team up with a booster who helps you win games and elevate your ranking. With Duo boost, you actively participate in the game alongside the booster. It is often preferred by players who want to learn from the booster and improve their own gameplay while climbing the ranks. On the other hand, solo boost is a more affordable option suitable for players who want to quickly enhance their ranking without actively playing the game.
